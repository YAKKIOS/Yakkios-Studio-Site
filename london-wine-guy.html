The London Wine Guy

Ross Johnson is a London based wine connoisseur showcasing his talents in the world of vino going by ‘The London Wine Guy’. As he looked to expand his reach and influence among the wine community, he approached me to create a brand identity that will help create a position for him to do this.

The challenge

In the era of a booming modern city contrasted against the tradition and legacy behind wine and wineries, finding a foothold among the world of vino is difficult. The challenge was to generate a brand identity that encapsulated both traditional and modern aspects.

The Solution

It was important to blend both modern and traditional aspects in to the brand. The traditional wine bottle shape is iconic and has stood the test of time from before the 1800s and is understood around the world. Tower Bridge, a famous London landmark can be recognised from the outline standing proud on the Thames. Combining these instantly creates a recognisable icon encapsulating the desired message. A modern yet bold typeface was used to outline the fast-paced city of London, the capitals were used to help resemble the famous ‘London Underground’ signs seen around the city.
