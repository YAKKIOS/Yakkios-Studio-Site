A new direction for beer discovery

Creating a visually appealing image based site for known, North Eastern based, photographer Tom Bell. My vision was to truly showcase the beauty of the photographs and to capture the same feeling of being inside a gallery. The content consists of images and little text to ensure attention is kept on the beautiful visuals within the photographs.

Understanding the Problem

I was able to understand the issue from a customers perspective as my persona fit. I also had interviews with other customers who fit within the personas identified. To gain additional research I interviewed a pub owner which allowed me to understand the problem from both sides. Paired with the competitor research I was able to identify commonalities between them and generate a solution towards this.
